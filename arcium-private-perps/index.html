ğŸ” Privacy Benefits

ğŸ”’ Private Positions

Position sizes and directions are encrypted using Arcium. Only the trader knows their exact position details. Market makers cannot see aggregate position data.

ğŸ”’ Private Orders

Orders are submitted as encrypted intents. Matching engine operates on encrypted data. Only execution results are revealed, preventing front-running.

ğŸ”’ Private Liquidation Checks

Health checks compute privately using Arcium. Liquidators cannot see which positions are at risk, preventing targeted liquidation attacks.

âœ… Transparent Settlement

Final PnL is computed and revealed on-chain. Ensures fair settlement while maintaining privacy of position details.
